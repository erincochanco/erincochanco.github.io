The Wikipedia exercise tasked us to reenvision an article on the site. I picked Émile Benveniste because, at the time, I encountered his name in a book and found his theories interesting. The site itself doesn't boast of visual strength as it mimics the layout of Wikipedia. Due to the topic's bookish nature, I applied a brownish neutral palette that references the "soft academia" aesthetic.
