   Interview
Project

For the first project of the term, we were tasked to create website that showcased an interview conducted between partners. I was partnered with Rand Rivera, who works as a part-time barista at Starbucks. My questions consequently focused on this aspect of his life.

The website reflected the central theme of the interview. For the color palette, green was primarily utilized as it referenced Starbucks' branding. I additionally illustrated icons that featured relevant visuals such as coffee cups, coffee beans and a barista apron.
